Overview

Perched at an impressive altitude of 14,000 feet, the Hampta Pass trek offers a captivating contrast between two striking landscapes. Nestled in the heart of Himachal Pradesh, India, this exhilarating journey begins in the lush green valleys of Kullu and ascends into the stark, rugged terrain of Lahaul. Trekkers are treated to spectacular views of the Dhauladhar mountain range, set against a vibrant backdrop of blooming wildflowers—making it an ideal escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. The trek provides a harmonious blend of natural charm, excitement, and panoramic vistas. Covering a distance of approximately 35 km over the course of 5 days, the trail winds through verdant meadows, thick forests, and steep ascents. From serene valleys and river crossings to dramatic high-altitude passes, the Hampta Pass trek transitions from Kullu’s lush greenery to Lahaul’s arid landscapes, culminating at 14,100 feet. It promises a truly surreal and unforgettable experience for thrill-seekers.

Itinerary

DAY 0 - Depart from Delhi

  • Depart from Delhi in the evening via bus/Volvo.
  • Overnight journey to Manali.
  • Prepare for the trek starting the next day.

DAY 1 - Manali to Jobra → Trek to Chika (10,100 ft)

  • Shared jeep ride from Manali bus stand to Jobra (trek starting point).
  • Trek to Chika (approx. 2 km) through forests of pine, maple, and birch along River Rani.
  • Altitude gain: up to 10,400 ft.
  • Scenic views of rocky Himachali terrain.
  • Overnight stay in tents at Chika campsite.

Day 2: Chika → Balu ka Ghera (11,900 ft)

  • Begin trek from Chika to Balu ka Ghera.
  • Witness outer Himalayan and Dhauladhar ranges.
  • Gradual altitude gain and changing landscapes.
  • Campsite at Balu ka Ghera, surrounded by mountain views.
  • Overnight stay in tents.

Day 3: Balu ka Ghera → Hampta Pass (14,100 ft) → Siagoru (12,900 ft)

  • Start at Balu ka Ghera (12,900 ft), ascend to Hampta Pass (14,100 ft), then descend to Siagoru (12,900 ft).
  • Approx. 8-hour trek with moderate difficulty.
  • Cross snowfields, glacial paths, and rocky ridges.
  • Interact with local shepherds en route.
  • Transition from green Hampta Valley to barren Lahaul Valley.
  • Overnight stay in tents at Siagoru.

Day 4: Siagoru → Chatru (11,000 ft) → Drive to Chandratal → Return to Chatru

  • 4-hour trek from Siagoru to Chatru via Pir Panjal range.
  • Enjoy desert-like landscapes of Spiti Valley.
  • Arrive at Chatru for lunch and rest.
  • 4-hour drive to Chandratal Lake (14,100 ft).
  • Explore the pristine, high-altitude lake.
  • Return drive to Chatru for overnight camping.

Day 5: Chatru → Manali → Delhi

  • Drive back to Manali via the Atal Tunnel.
  • Scenic route through valleys and mountains.
  • Arrive in Manali by evening.
  • Continue return journey to Delhi via overnight bus.

Inclusion

  • Accommodation: All essential camping gear like tents, sleeping bags, and mats will be provided. Tents are typically shared among 3–4 trekkers.
  • Meals:All meals during the trek (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and evening tea) are included. Food is simple, nourishing, and usually a mix of Indian and continental dishes.
  • Trekking Team: The package includes skilled trek leaders, experienced guides, and a support crew to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience..
  • Transport:Transportation from the starting point of the trek to the endpoint is covered in the package..
  • First Aid: A basic medical kit will be carried along for emergencies.

Exclusion

  • Purchases: Items like bottled water, energy drinks, snacks, and alcohol are not covered.
  • Travel Insurance: You are required to arrange your own travel insurance; it is not included.
  • Medical Evacuation: In case of emergencies requiring evacuation (like helicopter rescue), the cost must be borne by the trekker.
  • Gratuities:Tipping the trekking staff is optional and not part of the package.
  • Other Expenses: Any services not listed in the inclusions section are not part of the package.

Things To Carry

  • Backpack: A durable, comfortable backpack for carrying all essentials.
  • Clothing: Include warm layers and waterproof gear like thermals, fleece, jackets, gloves, and a woolen cap.
  • Footwear:Quality trekking shoes with grip, along with extra pairs of socks.
  • Hydration:Carry water bottles or a hydration bladder to stay well-hydrated throughout the trek.
  • Snacks:Bring dry snacks like energy bars, nuts, and dried fruits for extra energy.
  • Medical Kit: A personal first-aid kit with painkillers, bandages, and antiseptics.
  • Lighting:A headlamp or flashlight for visibility during evenings or in low light.
  • ID Proof:A valid government-issued ID (like Aadhar or Driver’s License) for identity verification.

CONFIRMATION POLICY

  • A booking confirmation voucher will be emailed within 24 hours of successful booking.
  • If your chosen date is unavailable, a new date as per your preference will be offered, and a revised confirmation will be sent.
  • You may cancel the booking before confirmation if rescheduling isn’t acceptable, and a full refund will be processed.

CANCELLATION POLICY

  • 0–15 days before start: No refund; 100% cancellation charges apply.

REFUND POLICY:

  • If you opt for a refund after cancellation, the amount will be credited back to the original payment method within 10–12 working days.

Trek Highlights

Stunning Natural Beauty at Hampta Pass Trek

  • Mesmerizing Landscapes: Witness awe-inspiring views of snow-covered Himalayan peaks, vibrant green valleys, and the crystal-clear blue waters of the enchanting Chandratal Lake.
  • Thrilling Adventure:A moderately challenging trek ideal for seasoned trekkers seeking both physical excitement and scenic rewards.
  • Abundant Wildlife:Encounter diverse Himalayan wildlife, including ibex, black bears, and even elusive snow leopards, along with rare bird species and unique alpine flora.
  • Discover Chandratal: Uncover the magic of Chandratal, the crescent-shaped "Lake of the Moon," known for its surreal beauty and color-shifting waters throughout the day.
